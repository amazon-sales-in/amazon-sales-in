Amazon Sales Dataset 2022 India

The Amazon Sales Dataset 2022 India is a richly enriched dataset simulating Indian ecommerce behavior, product reviews, transactions, and customer demographics. Based on authentic Amazon product and review information, this version has been cleaned and extended to support advanced analytics including segmentation, basket analysis, and text mining. Snapshot on 15th Sept 2022 of orders in top 9 metros in India.
